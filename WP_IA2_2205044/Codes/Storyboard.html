Color Rationale

  • Blue

    Lighter shades of blue can create a calm and serene atmosphere, making it suitable for my website that aim to relax my browsing visitors. Blue is also a color of huge versatility and promotes trust, confidence, and tranquility;
    Darker blues, such as navy or royal blue, often exude a sense of professionalism and seriousness and so this was used for my resumer as it is supportive in establishing a formal and credible image.

  • Black and White

    There were mainly used to support the background images

  • Sunset Background

    The dark sunset backgrounds was used create a visually striking and aesthetically pleasing look. The contrast between the dark foreground and the vibrant colors of the sunset can be used to captivate and engage visitors. It is also used to instill an emotional impact as sunset scenes tends to evoke a sense of calm, tranquility, and nostalgia and are capable of conveying emotions like serenity, relaxation, and introspection, which can be beneficial for my website as I aiming to create a certain mood for my readers.
